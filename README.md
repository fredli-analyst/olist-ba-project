# Olist E-Commerce — Business Analysis Portfolio Project

**Analyst:** Fred Li | Business Analyst | Entry Level Project  
**Dataset:** Olist Brazilian E-Commerce (2016–2018) | 100,000+ Orders

**Target Roles:** Business Analyst 

---

## Project Summary

This project presents a complete end-to-end Business Analysis of the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset. The objective was to identify the root causes of customer dissatisfaction, quantify operational risk, and deliver strategic recommendations using a structured BA methodology.

The analysis goes beyond surface-level findings to identify two independent drivers of negative reviews — a distinction that changes the entire strategic response.

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Data Point |
|---|---|
| Late delivery rate | 8.1% of all orders |
| 1-Star review rate | 11.9% (11,858 orders) |
| Correlation: Late delivery → 1-Star | 0.87 (strong) |
| Worst performing states | AL, MA, PI |
| Office Furniture 1-star rate (on-time orders) | 20.5% |
